Documentation

  • Country: The country where the weather observation is taking place.
  • Name: The name of the city or town where the weather observation is taking place.
  • Region: The region of the country where the weather observation is taking place.
  • Tz_id: The time zone identifier for the location of the weather observation.
  • Lat: The latitude of the location of the weather observation.
  • Localtime: The local time of the weather observation.
  • Localtime_epoch: The Unix epoch timestamp of the weather observation.
  • Lon: The longitude of the location of the weather observation.
  • Current: An object containing the current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, etc.
  • Cloud: The percentage of the sky that is covered by clouds.
  • Feelslike_c: The perceived temperature by humans.
  • Feelslike_f: The perceived temperature by humans in Fahrenheit.
  • Gust_kph: The peak wind gust speed in kilometers per hour.
  • Gust_mph: The peak wind gust speed in miles per hour.
  • Humidity: The percentage of water vapor in the air.
  • Precip_in: The amount of precipitation that has fallen in inches.
  • Precip_mm: The amount of precipitation that has fallen in millimeters.
  • Pressure_in: The air pressure in inches of mercury.
  • Pressure_mb: The air pressure in millibars.
  • Temp_c: The temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • Temp_f: The temperature in degrees Fahrenheit.
  • Uv: The ultraviolet index.
  • Vis_km: The visibility in kilometers.
  • Vis_miles: The visibility in miles.
  • Wind_degree: The direction of the wind in degrees.
  • Wind_dir: The direction of the wind as a compassdirection (e.g., north, south, east, west).
  • Wind_kph: The wind speed in kilometers per hour.
  • Wind_mph: The wind speed in miles per hour.
